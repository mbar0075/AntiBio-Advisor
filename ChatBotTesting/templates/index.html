Antibio Advisor

An intelligent interface project targeted towards educating people of different ages about when and why to use antibiotic medication.

Lets Chat!

In English

This website depicts an academic research project aimed towards reducing misinformation with regards to antibiotics and their use. In light of this this webpage only serves as a proof of concept thus, the implementation has been carried out in english given that english is Malta's second language and the main target audience is people aged 18 or over.

Disclaimer: The information provided to the user throughout the website although factual and correct, does not substite the medical opinion of a professional doctor. Please seek medical advice from trained individuals before attempting anything suggested in the website.
